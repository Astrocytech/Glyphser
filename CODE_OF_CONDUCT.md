# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that build an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating disagreement.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus discussions on technical and project outcomes.
- Respect different viewpoints, backgrounds, and communication styles.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes, and correct them quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or demeaning conduct of any kind.
- Personal attacks, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments.
- Sexual language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Threats, intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information (such as personal addresses or contact details) without explicit permission.
- Any behavior that would reasonably make community participation unsafe or hostile.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
- Repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews.
- Project chat channels and mailing lists.
- Public or private interactions when an individual is representing this project or its community.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it to the maintainers:

- Primary contact: `maintainers@astrocytech.com`
- Backup contact: open a private security report per `SECURITY.md` if email is unavailable.

Please include, when possible:
- What happened and when.
- Links, screenshots, or logs.
- People involved and any witnesses.
- Any immediate safety concerns.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take any action they consider appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior.

Possible enforcement actions include:
1. Private warning.
2. Temporary restriction from interactions.
3. Removal of content (comments, issues, or pull requests).
4. Temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Enforcement decisions will be based on impact, severity, pattern of behavior, and community safety.

## Appeals
If you believe an enforcement action was made in error, you may appeal by contacting `maintainers@astrocytech.com` with relevant context. Appeals will be reviewed by maintainers not directly involved in the original decision when possible.
