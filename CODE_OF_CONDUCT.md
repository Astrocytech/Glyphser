# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that build an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy project environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful, constructive, and professional.
- Give and receive feedback in a technically focused and civil manner.
- Respect different viewpoints, backgrounds, and communication styles.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them promptly.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its technical outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or demeaning conduct of any kind.
- Personal attacks, trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Sexual language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Threats, intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information, such as personal addresses or contact details,
  without explicit permission.
- Any behavior that would reasonably make participation unsafe, hostile, or
  professionally inappropriate.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
- Repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews.
- Any official project communication channels that may be established.
- Public interactions when an individual is officially representing this project.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct,
report it to the maintainers at:

- `maintainers@astrocytech.com`

Please include, where possible:
- What happened and when.
- Links, screenshots, or logs.
- People involved and any witnesses.
- Any immediate safety concerns.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as reasonably
possible.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of
Conduct and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

Possible enforcement actions include:
1. Private warning.
2. Temporary restriction from project interactions.
3. Removal of comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions.
4. Temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Enforcement decisions will be based on impact, severity, pattern of behavior,
and project safety.

## Appeals
If you believe an enforcement action was made in error, you may appeal by
contacting `maintainers@astrocytech.com`. Appeals will be reviewed by
maintainers not directly involved in the original decision when possible.
