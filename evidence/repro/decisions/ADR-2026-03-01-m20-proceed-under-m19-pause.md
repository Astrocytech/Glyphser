# ADR-2026-03-01: Milestone 20 Execution Under Milestone 19 Pause

Date: 2026-03-01
Status: Accepted (Temporary)
Owner: Astrocytech/Glyphser

## Context
Milestone 19 is paused due unavailable multi-OS hosts. Milestone 20 (language ecosystem expansion) can still progress on currently available hosts.

## Decision
Allow milestone 20 to proceed while milestone 19 remains BLOCKED/PAUSED.

## Constraints
- Milestone 19 remains mandatory for OS universality claims.
- Milestone 20 reports must include this waiver ADR in `waivers.json`.
- Global certification milestones remain gated by paused OS/device milestones.

## Closure
This ADR closes when milestone 19 resumes and reaches DONE.
