# ADR-2026-03-01: Milestone 25 Progress With Blocked Prerequisites

Date: 2026-03-01
Status: Accepted (Temporary)
Owner: Astrocytech/Glyphser

## Context
Milestone 25 requires completion of milestones 18-24 plus closure of milestones 12 and 16. Several prerequisites are currently blocked/paused due hardware and platform availability.

## Decision
Allow milestone 25 implementation work (certification aggregator, compatibility assembly, evidence schema) to proceed, while final PASS remains gated by prerequisite completion.

## Constraints
- Milestone 25 may remain IN_PROGRESS/BLOCKED until all prerequisite milestones are DONE.
- No universal/global certification claims are valid until this gate is fully satisfied.

## Closure
Close this ADR when milestone 25 reaches PASS with all prerequisite milestones DONE.
