# ADR-2026-03-01: Milestone 24 Progress With Limited Target Devices

Date: 2026-03-01
Status: Accepted (Temporary)
Owner: Astrocytech/Glyphser

## Context
Milestone 24 targets Android, iOS, and Web runtime coverage. Full hardware/runtime access may not be available at all times.

## Decision
Allow implementation and partial evidence generation for milestone 24 while final PASS remains gated by real target coverage.

## Constraints
- Milestone 24 can remain IN_PROGRESS/BLOCKED until all required targets are present.
- Milestone 25 remains gated by completion of milestone 24.

## Closure
Close this ADR once milestone 24 reaches PASS with Android, iOS, and Web target evidence.
