# Policies

Normative policy documents that govern compatibility, determinism, conformance, and error behavior.

## Scope
- Compatibility and versioning policy.
- Determinism and numeric policy.
- Conformance suite policy and error-code policy.
- Compatibility kits and certification criteria under `specs/policies/compatibility/`.

## Rules
- Policy files are normative and versioned.
- Policy references must resolve to canonical contracts/schemas in `specs/`.
- Product-facing operational policies belong in `product/handbook/policies/` and are non-normative for conformance.
