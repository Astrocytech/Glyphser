# Emergency Lockdown and Rollback Checklist

1. Confirm incident ticket is created and linked.
2. Apply signed emergency lockdown policy toggle with explicit expiry.
3. Verify lockdown blast-radius constraints are within policy limits.
4. Capture containment evidence artifact and operator approvals.
5. Validate no prohibited operations remain enabled during lockdown.
6. Prepare rollback candidate artifacts and verify provenance/signatures.
7. Execute rollback attestation verification gate.
8. Generate signed rollback completion artifact.
9. Restore normal policy state and verify expiration/closure of temporary exceptions.
10. Publish post-incident corrective actions and associated regression tests.
