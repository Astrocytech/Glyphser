# Contractual Support SLA

| Severity | Example | Initial Response | Mitigation Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sev-1 | Service unavailable, data loss risk | 1 hour | 24 hours |
| Sev-2 | Major feature blocked | 4 hours | 3 business days |
| Sev-3 | Minor defect/workaround exists | 1 business day | 10 business days |
| Sev-4 | Question/enhancement | 2 business days | Planned backlog |
