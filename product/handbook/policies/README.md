# Product Policies Scope

This folder contains product-facing operational and business policies.

## Included Here
- Release operations and communication policy.
- Support lifecycle and customer commitment policy.
- Review and governance process policy for operating the product.

## Excluded From Here
- Protocol, conformance, determinism, compatibility, and machine-checkable policy.
- Normative policy that is used by gates or conformance checks.

Normative technical policy belongs under `specs/policies/`.
